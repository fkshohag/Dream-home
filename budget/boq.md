## 15. Quick procurement checklist

- [ ] **Cement**: 459 bags. Buy in 2 lots — first 250 for foundation + columns, rest later.
- [ ] **MS rod**: 8.0 ton, Fe-500. Buy from BSRM / KSRM dealer. **Always demand mill test certificate.**
- [ ] **Brick**: 20,700 nos. 1st-class. Order in 3 lots to match construction phase.
- [ ] **Sand**: 2,100 cft Sylhet coarse. Stockpile in two heaps to avoid contamination.
- [ ] **Stone chips**: 1,180 cft (¾"). Test for chloride content if locally sourced.
- [ ] **Windows**: 12 nos. — get 3 vendor quotes; lead time 3 weeks.
- [ ] **Doors**: 11 nos. — segun + PVC. Local carpenter + 2 weeks lead.
- [ ] **Tiles**: ~ 1,560 sqft floor + 200 sqft wall. Buy 5 % extra.
- [ ] **Sanitary**: 2 baths + kitchen sink. Order 2 weeks before second-fix.
