## 3. Cost-Saving Levers

| Lever | Saving |
|---|---:|
| Buy steel/cement at slab-cast bulk rates | 3–5% |
| Direct material purchase (skip contractor markup) | 5–8% |
| Standard tile sizes (less wastage/cutting) | 1–2% |
| Phase finishes (occupy, finish rooms over time) | defer 10–15% |
| Self-supervise + day-labour vs full contract | 8–12% |
