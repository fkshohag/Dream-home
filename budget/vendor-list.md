## 7. Buying Tips

1. **Steel & cement** — biggest cost; buy at slab-cast volume for best rates.
2. Take **delivery challans** + weigh steel on arrival.
3. Store cement dry, off the floor; use within 60 days.
4. Test 1st-class brick: sharp edges, metallic ring, ≤ 15% water absorption.
5. Insist on **brand bags/tags** for cement & steel (avoid fakes).
