## 8. Approvals required (Union Parishad — rural)

1. **Land mutation document** (Khatian + Porcha)
2. **Architectural plan + structural drawings** (stamped by registered engineer)
3. **Soil test report** (auger to 10 ft, 3 points)
4. **Site plan** showing setbacks, access roads, drainage
5. **Estimated cost certificate**
6. **No-objection certificate** from neighbours (if within minimum side setback)
7. **Application form** + fee (typically BDT 5,000–15,000)

Expected approval time: **30–60 days**. Start the application in **Month -2** of your build schedule.

---

## 9. Bangladesh-specific provisions

- **Cyclone shelter band**: not mandatory for inland (Comilla / Dhaka district), but if coastal (Khulna / Barishal / Chattogram), reinforced corner walls + cyclone-rated roof anchors required.
- **Flood protection plinth**: plinth ≥ 2'-0" above HFL (Highest Flood Level on record); designed = 2'-6" ✓ for most central districts.
- **Salinity protection**: if within 30 km of coast, double waterproofing on plinth + chloride-resistant rebar coating required. **Not applicable here** (assumed central BD).
- **Rural electrification (REB)**: standard single-phase 5 kW connection. Solar net-metering allowed if certified.
