# Component Calculation — 48' × 25' Home (1,200 sqft)

> Quantity take-off and cost estimate for the core house (RCC frame + brickwork + finishes). Bangladesh 2026 mid-range rates. Boundary wall, tube well, deep interior fit-out, and landscape are listed separately at the end.

**Basis:**
- Footprint: 48'-0" × 25'-0" = 1,200 sqft (interior); verandah 25'×6' = 150 sqft projecting.
- Structure: RCC frame, 15 columns (3×5 grid: 12'-6" E-W × 12'-0" N-S).
- Heights: plinth 2'-6"; floor→roof 10'-6"; footing founded 4'-6" below NGL.
- Conversions: 1 m³ = 35.31 cft; 1 bag cement = 50 kg; brick (1st class) ≈ 13.5 nos/cft of masonry.
