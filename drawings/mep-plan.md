# MEP Plan — Plumbing, Electrical, Sanitary (48' × 25' Home)

> Mechanical / Electrical / Plumbing design for the finalized layout: 3 baths (2 attached + 1 common), kitchen on the north wall, water tanks on roof, deep tube well + septic tank/soak pit. Future-2nd-floor risers provided.

---

## A. PLUMBING — Water Supply

```
   Deep tube well ──► submersible pump ──► UNDERGROUND reservoir (500 gal)
                                                  │
                                          transfer pump
                                                  │
                                          ROOF tank 2×1000 L
                                                  │ (gravity)
        ┌──────────────┬──────────────┬──────────┴───────┐
     Kitchen        Att Bath 2     Att Bath 3        Common Bath
```

| Item | Spec |
|---|---|
| Source | Deep tube well, 4" bore, ~250–350 ft (confirm local aquifer) |
| Pump | 1.0 HP submersible |
| Underground reservoir | 500 gal RCC, watertight, NE corner of plot |
| Roof tank | 2 × 1000 L PE on 8'×8' RCC platform |
| Supply pipe | uPVC/CPVC: 1" main riser, ¾" branch, ½" fixtures |
| Hot water | Instant geyser points in 2 master baths |
| Future | Cap a riser tee at roof for 2nd-floor supply |

### Fixture supply summary

| Att Bath 2 | WC, basin, shower, geyser |
| Att Bath 3 | WC, basin, shower, geyser |

## B. PLUMBING — Drainage & Sanitation

```
   Baths/Kitchen ──► soil & waste pipes ──► inspection pits ──► SEPTIC TANK ──► SOAK PIT
       (4" soil / 2" waste, uPVC, slope 1:40)        │                    │
                                                gas vent              overflow to ground
```

| Item | Spec |
|---|---|
| Soil pipe (WC) | 4" uPVC, vent to roof |
| Waste pipe (basin/sink/shower) | 2" uPVC with traps |
| Slope | 1:40 to 1:50 toward septic |
| Septic tank | 2-chamber RCC, **8'×4'×5'** (≈ 6–7 person), buried, rear/north yard |
| Soak pit | 5' dia × 6' deep, brick honeycomb + boulder/charcoal |
| Inspection pits | at each junction + before septic, 18"×18" with cover |
| Kitchen | grease trap before joining waste line |
| Rainwater | separate (see roof-plan) — never into septic |

> Septic tank ≥ 5' from any structure foundation and ≥ 50' from the tube well. Soak pit downstream of (away from) the tube well.

## D. Gas / Ventilation

- **Kitchen** on the north external wall → window + **exhaust fan** vents cooking heat/smoke directly outdoors (the key reason it's wall-mounted).
- Gas: LPG cylinder (double-burner) or piped gas if available; provide a ventilated cylinder niche.
- All baths: exhaust fan + high-level louver.
- Cross-ventilation: every habitable room has windows on **two** external walls.
