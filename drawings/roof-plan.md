# Roof Plan — 48' × 25' Single-Story Home

> Flat RCC roof over the 48'×25' footprint with parapet, water tanks, solar array, roof garden, future-stair plug and rainwater drainage. Designed to also be the floor slab of a future 2nd floor.

---

## 1. Roof Layout

```
                          NORTH
   ┌──────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │ ◐ downspout                    downspout ◑│
   │   ┌────────────┐                          │
   │   │ ROOF GARDEN │      ┌───────────────┐  │
   │   │  12' × 8'    │      │ WATER TANK    │  │
   │   │  planters    │      │ 2×1000 L on   │  │
   │   └────────────┘      │ 8'×8' RCC plat.│  │
   │                        └───────────────┘  │
   │   ┌──────────┐                            │
   │   │ FUTURE    │      [ 6 × solar panels ] │
   │   │ STAIR plug│      [ 2.4 kW, tilt 15°S ]│
   │   │  8' × 5'  │                           │
   │ ◐ downspout                    downspout ◑│
   └──────────────────────────────────────────┘
                          SOUTH (verandah cantilever below)
      48' (N–S) × 25' (E–W)  ·  slope 1:80 to 4 corner downspouts
```

| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Roof slab | 5" RCC, M20 (doubles as future floor) |
| Parapet | 3'-6" tall, 5" brick, weep holes |
| Slope | 1:80 toward 4 corner downspouts (4" uPVC) |
| Water tanks | 2 × 1000 L on 8'×8' RCC platform (NE quadrant) |
| Solar | 6 × 400 W = 2.4 kW, GI frame, tilt ~15° south |
| Roof garden | 12'×8' raised RCC planter, HDPE liner, 6" soil, drain |
| Future stair opening | 8'×5' precast plug slab (aligned over stair below) |
| Waterproofing | 2" brick-bat coba + net cement finish + terracotta tile |

---

## 2. Rainwater & Drainage

- 4 corner downspouts (4" uPVC) carry roof water down.
- Two downspouts feed the **rainwater harvesting tank** (see `drawings-bangla/landscape-rainwater.md`); overflow to soak pit.
- Parapet weep holes prevent ponding at the edge.
- Roof-garden planter has its own drain to the nearest downspout (never onto the slab).

---

## 3. Future 2nd-Floor Notes

- The roof slab is already designed as a **floor slab** for Phase-2.
- Water-tank platform + solar frames are **relocatable** when the 2nd floor is built.
- Stair-plug slab is removed in Phase-2 to continue the stair upward.
- Column starter bars (3'-0" above slab) are protected until then.

---

## 4. Roof Access

- Via the internal stair to a small RCC hatch/landing (lockable).
- Parapet 3'-6" high for safety; add a 6" coping.
