## 2. New home position & setbacks

| Boundary | Setback | Comment |
|---|---:|---|
| South (compound side, toward flower bagan) | 25–30 ft | Generous — flower bagan acts as front garden buffer |
| East (own boundary wall) | 8–10 ft | Adequate for windows + service strip |
| North (NE / NW old homes) | 10–15 ft | Privacy via high-sill windows |
| West (internal road + tin shed) | 5–7 ft to road; ~5 ft to tin shed | TIGHT — see SW screen-wall note below |

> **BNBC**: minimum setbacks for low-rise residential (rural) are typically
> Front 10' / Rear 5' / Sides 4'. All sides comfortably meet code.

## 3. Approach paths

### Primary (formal): through the new home's MAIN GATE (west)
```
Internal road → MAIN GATE (sliding ornamental metal, 8'-0" wide)
              → small front mini-yard (cropped lawn, marigolds, potted palms)
              → 5'-0" wide red-brick walkway with one landscape lamp halfway
              → step up 1'-6" to verandah floor
              → 4'-0" segun front door (D1)
              → drawing room
```

### Secondary (utility / family): through the OUTER COMPOUND GATE (south)
```
South public road → outer compound gate
                  → past car garage (E) and flower bagan (centre)
                  → narrow brick walkway turning around the east of new home
                    OR past the SW tin shed (along the internal road)
                  → reach the SOUTH back door (D2) directly into stair space
```

The south back door is for **kitchen deliveries that come via the south gate,
family members, gardeners working in flower bagan, evening compound walks.**

## 5. Drainage & utilities

| Service | Location | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Septic tank | NE corner of compound, ~ 15 ft from new home, ~ 10 ft from any old home | 1,500 L 3-chamber RCC septic with soak well |
| Underground reservoir | SW corner outside new home, 1,000 L RCC | Filled from existing pond / municipal supply / hand pump |
| Submersible pump | 1 HP, in valve chamber beside reservoir | Lifts water to roof tanks |
| Storm drainage | 4" PVC down-spouts at four corners of roof; surface gradient toward NE corner | Slope ~ 1:80; discharges to compound soak pit |
| Electrical meter | West boundary wall, near main gate | Standard rural single-phase 5 kW; spare conduit for 3-phase upgrade |
| Solar inverter location | Inside stair-space (R10) | Cool, dry; cables drop from roof through wall chase |

## 6. Plot context summary

- **Location**: rural Bangladesh family compound, multiple existing homes preserved.
- **Existing structures retained**: 1× car garage (SE), 1× SW tin-shed home, 2× outer NE & NW old homes, flower bagan landscape.
- **New construction**: 1× modern single-story RCC house, designed for future 2-storey expansion.
- **Total compound footprint usage** (rough): existing ~ 1,200 sqft + new ~ 1,400 sqft + walkways/yards ≈ 5,500 sqft on plot.
