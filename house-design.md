# 🏠 Dream Home — Project Index

**Modern single-story 1,314 sqft village house, Bangladesh.**
Designed for the family compound with provision for a future 2-storey extension.

> One-line summary: 36'-6" × 36'-0" single-story RCC house, 3 bedrooms,
> west-facing formal entry, **~ BDT 47 lakh (mid budget) for Phase-1**,
> + ~ BDT 36 lakh for Phase-2 (1st floor) when the family is ready.

## 🧭 Site & orientation

- **Public road**: South (compound has its own outer gate here)
- **Internal road**: West (5–7 ft wide, pedestrian + bicycle); new home's **main gate is here**
- **Formal entry**: West verandah → drawing room
- **Service entry**: South back door → stair-space utility area
- **Boundary**: East (own land, most private; biggest windows here)
- **Neighbours**: SW low tin shed (~5 ft, screened by decorative wall); NE & NW older brick-tin homes (~10-15 ft, high-sill windows for privacy)

See `drawings/site-plan.md` and `renders/site/site-plan-illustration.png` for the full layout.

## 🛠 How to use these documents

### If you are the OWNER
1. Read this file (`house-design.md`) for the big picture.
2. Open `drawings/floor-plan.md` to visualize the rooms.
3. Open `budget/cost-estimate.md` to set your budget; then `budget/cashflow.md` to time the money.
4. Browse the renders in `renders/` to feel the home.

### If you are the CONTRACTOR / mistri
1. Start with `drawings/structural-plan.md` for the structural design.
2. Use `budget/boq.md` for material quantities + pricing.
3. Follow `docs/construction-sequence.md` week-by-week.
4. Refer to `docs/materials-spec.md` for engineering specs.
5. Check `docs/bnbc-compliance.md` at each major hold-point.

### If you are the STRUCTURAL ENGINEER
1. Review `drawings/structural-plan.md` — verify member sizing for your local soil + seismic conditions.
2. Confirm Phase-2 provisions in `docs/phase-2-extension.md`.
3. Stamp the structural drawings before submission to Union Parishad.

### If you are the BANK / LENDER
1. Read `budget/cost-estimate.md` and `budget/cashflow.md`.
2. Use the milestone payment schedule for loan disbursement timing.

## 📞 Recommended next actions

1. **Soil test** — auger to 10 ft at 3 points. ~BDT 10,000. Cannot start work without this.
2. **Hire a structural engineer** in your district to review + stamp the drawings.
3. **Get 2–3 contractor quotes** using `budget/boq.md` as the tender document.
4. **Apply to Union Parishad** for building approval (30–60 day lead time).
5. **Secure liquid funds for first 2 months** (~BDT 13–15 lakh) before breaking ground.
